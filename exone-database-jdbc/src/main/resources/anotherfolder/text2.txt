GitLab Inc. is a company based on the GitLab open-source project. 
GitLab is an integrated product that unifies issues, code review, 
CI and CD into a single UI. GitLab Inc. offers self hosted products 
and SaaS plans for GitLab.com. GitLab is an open source project 
with a large community. Over 1800 people worldwide have contributed 
to GitLab! GitLab Inc. is an active participant in this community, 
see our stewardship of GitLab CE for more information. For a more 
customer focused overview see our pitches. We have a lot 
of information available online, see the primer for a good start.
